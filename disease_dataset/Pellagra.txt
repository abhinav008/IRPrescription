DrugId:  1
1. Name:  Niacin
2. Groups:  Approved, Investigational, Nutraceutical
3. Description:  A water-soluble vitamin of the B complex occurring in various animal and plant tissues. It is required by the body for the formation of coenzymes NAD and NADP. It has pellagra-curative, vasodilating, and antilipemic properties. [PubChem]
4. Indication:  For the treatment of type IV and V hyperlipidemia. It is indicated as ajunctive therapy.
