1. Name:  Flavin adenine dinucleotide
2. Groups:  Approved
3. Description:  A condensation product of riboflavin and adenosine diphosphate. The coenzyme of various aerobic dehydrogenases, e.g., D-amino acid oxidase and L-amino acid oxidase.
