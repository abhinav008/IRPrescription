DrugId:  1
1. Name:  Poliovirus type 1 antigen (formaldehyde inactivated)
2. Groups:  Approved, Investigational
3. Description:  Poliovirus type 1 antigen is a suspension of poliovirus Type 1 (Mahoney) used in the active immunization of infants (as young as 6 weeks of age), children, and adults for the prevention of poliomyelitis caused by poliovirus Type 1. The vaccine contains purified and inactivated poliovirus type 1 that were grown from a continuous line of monkey kidney cells.
4. Indication:  Not Available
