1. Name:  Measles virus strain enders' attenuated edmonston live antigen
2. Groups:  Approved, Investigational
3. Description:  Measles virus strain enders' attenuated edmonston live antigen is a live virus vaccine for simultaneous vaccination against measles, which is a common childhood disease. The vaccine is prepared from the attenuated line of measles virus, derived from Enders' attenuated Edmonston strain and propagated in chick embryo cell culture.
