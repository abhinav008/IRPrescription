DrugId:  1
1. Name:  Human Serum Albumin
2. Groups:  Approved
3. Description:  Human serum albumin isolated from expired blood plasma. In the iodonated form ( I 131), it is used as a diagnostic radiopharmaceutical for intravenous imaging.
4. Indication:  For treatment of severe blood loss, hypervolemia, and hypoproteinemia.
